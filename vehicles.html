Vehicles

Vehicles are the best way to quickly move around the map in PUBG Mobile. Although their loud engine noise undeniably draws attention to you, vehicles play a vital role to being successful in the game. While driving a vehicle, unless you take a direct headshot, you wonâ€™t incur a significant amount of damage from your opponents while moving. If the vehicle take too much damage, the vehicle will explode and instantly kill all passengers and players who stand in close. If the tires take too much damage, they will pop, reducing speed and handling performance.

Pickup

Pickups can be found in Miramar and Sanhok.

Driver and the front seat passenger is only protected in front doors, while the others two passenger will be fully exposed to outside world. Pickups are not easy to drive, it has quite high sensitivity.

There are two models in the Pickup section: pickup (large) and Rony.

Pros:

  • High sensitivity
  • Heavy, which makes it acquires high inertia

Cons:

  • Low acceleration
  • No protection for backseat passengers
  • Not easy to drive, need practice

Sedan

Sedans can be found in Erangel, Miramar and Vikendi.

It is an average kind of vehicle. Passengers are protected by front and rear doors. Since sedans have low bottoms, they have the best performance on the flat road.

There are three models in the Sedan section: Dacia, Mirado (closed top) and Mirado (open top).

Note: Mirados can only be found in Miramar.

Pros:

  • Good performance on the flat road
  • Relatively good durability

Cons:

  • Poor tractions
  • More susceptible to rollovers

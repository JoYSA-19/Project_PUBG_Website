Classic

The classic mode is the first mode that was introduced to players. The rules are simple:
  1. You can knock other players out of the game with weapons;
  2. Stay inside of the play zone, or you will lose health;
  3. If you have any teammates, you can be revived after when you are knocked out;
  4. Survive till the last one/team out of 100 players in one game.
Multiplayer:
  • Solo (1 player)
  • Duo (2 players)
  • Squad (4 players)

Arcade

The length of the game of Arcade modes are 8-15 min, they can be considered as combat practice to a specific skill.

Rules are vary from each mode.

Quick Match

Mode info: 8-Minute Matches with a random variation: all weapons, shotguns, melee, pistols, item heaven.

The quick match has no special rules, 28 players join in and have intense gunfight till the last one.

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok.
(The area of the game will be limited to the 1st safe zone)

Sniper Training

Mode info: 15-Minute Sniper Training: All Snipers rifles or Only Bolt Action Sniper Rifles.

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok.
(The area of the game will be limited to the 1st safe zone)

War

Mode info: Parachute with weapons and endless respawn.

Earn 3 points when killing an enemy and 1 point when reviving a teammate. Reach the specified points first to win!

Maps: Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok.
(The area of the game will be limited to the safe zone)

Arena

The Arena mode uses whole different maps compares to others.

Rules are vary from each mode.

Arena Training

This mode has infinite respawn. Eight players will be split up into two teams, 4 players on each side. Weapons can be picked up from the two spawning spot. One kill get 1 point to the team. In the given 8 minutes, first team which gets to 40 points wins.

Map: Warehouse.

Weapons available:
  • AR: M762, AKM, M416
  • SMG: Uzi, vector, Thompson
  • Sniper Rifle: Kar98K
  • Shotgun: S12K
  • Other: Frag Grenade, Stun Grenade

Team Deathmatch

This mode has infinite respawn. The rule is same as Arena Training, whatâ€™s different is that the weapons will not spawn at the battle ground. It is replaced by a weapon system called Loadout.

Map: Warehouse.

Domination

This mode has infinite respawn. Players can only use their weapon in the Loadout system.

The map has 3 bases, the first team to capture 2 bases wins. Once bases are activated, rush to occupy the base until capture. Super Crates will be available during the match.

Map: Town.

Loadout

The Loadout is a weapon preset system. The player is allowed to choose 4 combination of weapons for them to play in the Arena mode. Each set contains 4 slots: Main Weapon, Secondary Weapon, Explosive and Tactical. For weapons which can use attachments. The player needs to level up the weapons by using them in the matches.

Main weapons:

AR, SMG, Bolt Action Sniper Rifle, Automatic Sniper Rifle and Light Machine Gun. All weapons that can be found in the battlefield of Classic Mode are available. (Air drop weapons are not included)

Secondary Weapons:
  • Guns: P18C, Sawed-off, Skorpion, Desert Eagle
  • Melee: Machete, Pan

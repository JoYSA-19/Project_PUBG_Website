Tips

All tips here are based on my own gaming experience. It can be considered as a reference to new players or someone who wants to improve their skills. Some of the tips are still useful for those who had already played for long period of time.

Before Playing

Game setting is important to all players and it is often unique based on individual preferences.

Basic Setting

Aiming Assist
This is an automatic aiming assist completed by the device itself. Once you turn it on, you will feel little drag when aiming enemies. It is pretty useful when you shooting in a long range.
Block Sight Warning
A warning sign will show up instead of aiming dot when resisted by obstacles. Gun shots will fire to the ground if start firing.
Bolt-Action Rifle & Crossbow Firing Mode and Shotgun Firing mode
“Tap” is shooting instantly when you touch the firing button; when “Release” is on, touching and pressing the firing button will not fire, and when release the button, the gun will fire. I prefer the later one, depends on you.
Peek & Fire
When enable, you can lean your upper body to aim the enemy without your whole body is exposed. Highly Recommended.
Peek & Open Scope
When enable, open scope automatically when you peek. Highly Recommended.
Lean mode
Tap: tap the button to peek; Hold: hold the button to peek and aim, release to cancel; Mixed: tap to peek, hold for more than 0.3 seconds to keep peeking and aim, release to cancel.
Scope mode
Tap: tap the button to open scope; Hold: hold the button to keep the scope open and aim, release to cancel; Mixed: tap to open scope, hold for more than 0.3 seconds to keep the scope open and aim, release to cancel.
Canted Sight Button
See the .gif below ⬇️

Tap to use scope: the button for canted sight scope is an instant button, open when pressing the button.


Tap to switch scope: button is a switch, tap the “canted sight scope” switch first, then open the scope.
Auto open/close door
Door open automatically when running into the door, close when come out.
Healing Prompt
Enable to get the best recovery option based on health.
For example, if your health is below 75%, the first priority of medicine pane will be First Aid pack or Bandage (which heals you up to 75%); if higher than 75%, the first priority of medicine pane will be Energy Drink or Painkiller (energy boost consumables).
Jump/Climb
Combined button or two separate buttons for Jump and Climb (over walls).
Slide
It is only available in the Arena mode, try it yourself and it will surprise you. (Using method: press lie down button when running). Highly Recommended.
Damage Effect
The effect of “blood bursting” when you damage an enemy, you can make it red or green.
Universal Mark
A pretty useful quick communication tool. (Highly Recommended)
Two functioning:

Tap: tap a location to notice teammates; tap a vehicle; tap a supply.

Hold and slide: a quick communication wheel appears, or just quickly swipe the “Universal Mark” button to any direction for best communicating efficiency. (Need to remember the information on the wheel)
FPP Swap
Enable a quick switch button shows in your control pad, switch from Third point of view to First Point of view.

Control Pad

Design your own control pad based on your own preference. Can be modified during the game or outside of the game. Tap on a button to change the size and opacity. You can save your layouts online, 3 for TPP (3rd point of view) and 3 for FPP.

Sensitivity

Sensitivity is a vital element when playing the game, it will effect how fast you can swipe the screen to aim the enemy.

There’s three preset combination for players: low, medium and high. Try them all and choose one for yourself. If you want to customize some or all of them, you are free to do so.

Jump and Parachuting

First of all, put a mark of destination on the map before you get on the plane, discuss it with your teammates. You can zoom in & out the map.

I concluded three method for jump and parachuting for best efficiency.

  1. Near (800m and smaller)
  2. Mid (800m - 1100m)
  3. Far (1100m - 1500m)

Near

Be ready and jump at 800m, apply the following method immediately: face down and apply fast drop (left joystick: destination on the left -> left up 45˚ / destination on the right -> right up 45˚). Punch when landing to avoid rollover.

Mid

Jump at 1100m. Fly horizontally and switch to fast drop (left joystick: destination on the left -> left up 45˚ / destination on the right -> right up 45˚) at 400m-450m. Punch when landing to avoid rollover.

Far

Jump at 1500m. Fly horizontally for the whole time in the air. Punch when landing to avoid rollover.


Mr. Y’s saying to the rookies

  1. Use earphones or headsets. (Must-have accessory playing PUBG Mobile).
  2. Never focus on the loot when you are looting, instead, listen up and be cautious.
  3. Do not stand still at where you are when you are looting and ALWAYS get ready to move.
  4. When your teammates are under attack, stop looting immediately and help them as soon as you can (unless you don’t have enough weapons for helping).
  5. Hide your body when you are reloading your weapon.
  6. If you fire out of your whole current ammo pack in a close-ranged assault, switch to another gun (including pistols) or melee weapon.
  7. Do not chat too much when enemies are around.
  8. Respect and help your teammates, don’t be the one to poison the gaming environment.
  9. Good luck and have a chicken dinner!

AMM
Ammunition Type: The type of ammunition that the weapon accepts.
DMG
Hit Damage: The direct damage before applying other mitigating factors.
MAG
Ammo Per Mag: Maximum amount of ammo can be held in the magazine.
TMG
Time for Full Mag: The shooting duration for the whole mag of ammo.
SPD
Initial Bullet Speed: Higher number means less lead required against distant moving targets.
